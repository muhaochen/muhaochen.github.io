I am currently working with Prof. Dan Roth as a Postdoctoral Fellow. I have received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from UCLA, where I worked with Professors Carlo Zaniolo, Kai-Wei Chang, Wei Wang and Yizhou Sun. My research focuses on data-driven machine learning approaches for processing structured and unstructured data, and extending their applications to natural language understanding, knowledge base construction, computational biology and medical informatics. Particularly, I am interested in developing knowledge-aware learning systems with generalizability and requiring minimal supervision. Before joining UCLA as a Ph.D. student, I graduated with a Bachelor degree from Fudan University in 2014 where I worked with Prof. X. Sean Wang.
